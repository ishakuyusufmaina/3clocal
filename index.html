3 Center Game

3 Center Game Rules

Overview
3 Center is a two-player game played on a 3x3 board. Each player takes turns and uses a unique seed ("X" or "O") to play. A player can:

  • Place a seed on an empty cell.
  • Select one of their own seeds on the board.
  • Move a selected seed to another cell.

Game Constraints

  1. General Rules
    • A player can only play during their turn.
    • A player can only interact with their own seeds.
  2. Placing a Seed
    • A player may only place a seed if they have fewer than 3 seeds on the board.
    • The chosen cell must be empty.
  3. Selecting a Seed for Moving
    • A player may only select a seed if it belongs to them.
    • The player must already have 3 seeds on the board.
  4. Moving a Seed
    • The seed to be moved must be selected.
    • The target cell must be empty and adjacent to the selected cell.

Winning Condition
A player wins by arranging their 3 seeds in a centered line—vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
